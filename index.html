I'm an AI Engineer and M.Sc. candidate in Artificial Intelligence at Azad University, Tehran, with a 4.0 GPA. My work spans the full spectrum from research to production — designing computer vision models, building multi-agent LLM systems, and shipping generative AI pipelines.
